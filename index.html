World class instructor

At MMA Dallas, Valentin Espiricueta heads the instruction with more than 40 years of experience in martial arts. His expertise include Kali, Muy Thai, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Boxing, JKD and many more. Valentin trained under the world-renown Dan Inosanto and now teaches his experience to others.

No experience required

Get in shape

Fitness is important to us. Learning self-defense not only protects you, but it also gets you in shape quickly. We know how to train, and we do it well. If you're looking to lose weight, this is the place to be.
